FAQs

Do you operate 24/7?

Yes. Response times vary by area and time of day.

Which vehicles can you program?

Most common makes from ~2005+. Share your year/make/model to confirm support and chip type.

Can you help if I lost all keys (AKL)?

Often yes. Availability depends on the vehicle security system and parts on hand. We’ll confirm on the call.

Do you require proof of ownership?

Yes. A valid photo ID and proof you have the right to access the vehicle or property are required for service.

How long does programming take?

Typically 15–45 minutes; some immobilizer systems require longer security procedures.

How do you price jobs?

All quotes are given over the phone after verifying details (vehicle, key type, location). No online pricing to avoid surprises.

What’s your warranty?

90‑day parts & labor warranty against defects. Does not cover damage from misuse or unrelated vehicle issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

Card, tap‑to‑pay, and cash. Invoices available on request.

Where do you serve?

DMV — focused on Fairfax County, Virginia and surrounding areas
